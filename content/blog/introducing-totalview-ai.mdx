# Why We Built TotalView AI

Every contact centre has the same problem: you can only manually review a tiny fraction of your calls. Industry standard is 2-5%. That means 95-98% of your customer interactions go completely unexamined.

What's hiding in those calls? Compliance violations. Coaching opportunities. Churn signals. Competitor mentions. Moments of exceptional service that deserve recognition.

## The Old Way Doesn't Scale

Hiring more QA analysts is expensive (£35-45k each) and still can't achieve 100% coverage. Manual reviewers are inconsistent — two reviewers evaluating the same call will often disagree. And the process is slow: by the time you catch an issue, it's been happening for weeks.

## Enter TotalView AI

TotalView AI automatically transcribes, evaluates, and extracts intelligence from **100% of your calls**. Here's what that means:

- **99.2% transcription accuracy** with speaker diarisation and sentiment detection
- **Custom quality checks** defined in plain English — no templates or rigid forms
- **Smart review queue** that surfaces calls that need human attention
- **AI Copilot** for natural language queries across your entire call dataset

## Getting Started

Start with 10 free hours — no credit card required. Upload a few calls, define your quality criteria, and see what you've been missing.

We built this for QA managers, operations directors, compliance officers, and training leaders who are tired of flying blind. We hope it helps you as much as it would have helped us.
